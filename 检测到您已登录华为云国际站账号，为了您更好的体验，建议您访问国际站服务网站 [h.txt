# Data Encryption Workshop (DEW)

Data Encryption Workshop (DEW) is a full-stack data encryption service. It covers Key Management Service (KMS) and Dedicated Hardware Security Module (Dedicated HSM). With DEW, you can develop customized encryption applications, and integrate it with other Huawei Cloud services to meet even the most demanding encryption scenarios.
Data Encryption Workshop (DEW) is a full-stack encryption service. It covers Key Management Service (KMS) and Dedicated HSM.
